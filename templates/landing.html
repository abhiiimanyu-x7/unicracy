The Problem

Traditional Elections Are Broken

Paper ballots, long queues, counting errors, and zero transparency. Campus elections deserve a modern solution.

No Transparency

Students have no way to verify that their votes are counted correctly or that the process is fair.

Inefficient Process

Paper-based systems waste time, resources, and require manual counting that delays results for days.

Security Gaps

Duplicate voting, ballot stuffing, and lack of authentication make traditional elections vulnerable.

Features

Everything You Need for Fair Elections

Built from the ground up to make campus elections simple, secure, and transparent.

One Vote Lock

Every student gets exactly one vote per election. Once cast, it's locked — no duplicates, no manipulation.

Election Control Room

Admins get a powerful dashboard to create, manage, activate, and close elections with real-time stats.

Manifesto Profiles

Each candidate gets a rich profile card with their vision, qualifications, and election manifesto.

Result Analytics

Live results with animated charts, percentages, winner announcements, and participation statistics.
